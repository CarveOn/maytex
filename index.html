Intro 01 / 08

We make complex projects happen on site.

Commercial furniture projects rarely fail on one big thing. They fail on the moving parts: late deliveries, tight access, missing manpower, unclear sequencing, contractor delays, live buildings and deadlines that cannot move.

Maytex manages those moving parts. We provide the logistics, qualified manpower and site coordination to remove old furniture, install new furniture, support contractors and keep the job moving from start to finish.

About 07 / 08
Every detail, carefully handled.

Throughout his career Barry has been the person clients call when something big has to happen on a fixed date and cannot go wrong. He has worked for and with large organisations on jobs where the volume is high and the timing is fixed: hundreds of hotel rooms stripped and refitted with new beds, retail displays installed across multiple sites to a single deadline. Work where the plan, the people and the timing have to be exact, because there is no second attempt.

That is what Barry is built for. He is relentlessly organised, the operator who has the move sequenced, the crew briefed and the logistics solved before others have finished the first meeting. Clients stay with him because he is dependable in the way that matters most: he does what he said he would, on the day he said he would do it.

Today Barry is bringing that same control to the whole project, not just the heavy lifting. Office relocations managed end to end. Furniture sourced and procured. Contractors coordinated to one plan with one point of accountability. Same operator, same standards, more of the job handled for you.

Through Maytex, Barry leads a flexible, scalable team of qualified, safety trained people who deliver projects of every size. When the brief is complicated and the margin for error is zero, Barry and his team are the ones who make it happen.

A career spent moving, installing and delivering commercial projects that cannot go wrong. Organised, dependable, done.
